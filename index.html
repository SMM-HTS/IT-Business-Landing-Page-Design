Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Commenter AI

How does Commenter AI differ from other AI tools?

Commenter AI is specifically trained on LinkedIn engagement patterns and focuses on creating human-like, contextual comments rather than generic responses. Our technology understands professional nuances and helps you build authentic relationships, not just spam comments.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for all plans. You can test all features during this period with no credit card required. After the trial, you can choose to continue with a paid plan or cancel anytime.

Can I customize the tone of the comments?

Absolutely. You can set your preferred tone (professional, casual, enthusiastic, etc.), writing style, and even specific phrases you commonly use. The more you use Commenter AI, the better it learns to mimic your unique voice.

How do you ensure comments don't sound robotic?

Our AI incorporates natural language variations, emotional intelligence markers, and contextual understanding to create comments that feel genuine. We also provide multiple options so you can choose the one that sounds most like you.
